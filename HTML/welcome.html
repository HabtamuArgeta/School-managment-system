Here is the basic gaide how to use the system

In order to get Ethiopian University Exit Exam Questions

  1. click on exam button at the top center of the page
  2. Select your stream (natural, social, health, informatics, or engineering)
  3. Select your department from the left side menus
  4. Click on Exit Exam button
  5. Select the year at which the Exam you want is given
  6. Scroll up till you get the question you want

In order to get Addis Ababa University Model Exam Questions

  1. click on exam button at the top center of the page
  2. Select your stream (natural, social, health, informatics, or engineering)
  3. Select your department from the left side menus
  4. Click on Model Exam button
  5. Select the year at which the Exam you want is given
  6. Scroll up till you get the question you want
